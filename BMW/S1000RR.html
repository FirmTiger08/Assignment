BMW S1000RR

Price: ₹20,75,000 (Standard)

The BMW S 1000 RR is a super bike available in 3 variants: Standard, Pro, and Pro M Sport, with prices starting at ₹20,75,000. The other variants are priced at ₹23,10,000 and ₹25,25,000, respectively.

Powered by a 999cc BS6 engine, the BMW S 1000 RR develops a power of 206.51 bhp and a torque of 113 Nm. Equipped with both front and rear disc brakes, it features an anti-lock braking system. The bike weighs 197 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 16.5 liters.

Specifications:

  • Engine Capacity: 999 cc
  • Weight: 197 kg
  • Transmission: 6 Speed Manual
  • Seat Height: 780 mm
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 16.5 litres
