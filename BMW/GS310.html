BMW G310 GS

Price: ₹3,30,000

The BMW G 310 GS is an adventure bike available in only 1 variant and 4 colours. Powered by a 313cc BS6 engine, it develops a power of 33.52 bhp and a torque of 28 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, it comes equipped with an anti-lock braking system. Weighing 175 kg, it has a fuel tank capacity of 11.5 liters.

The 2022 BMW G 310 GS adventure-tourer recently received styling updates in the form of new paint themes. The latest iteration of the motorcycle is available in three colours – Cosmic Black 3, Style Rallye Kalamata Metallic Matte, and Racing Blue Metallic.

Specifications:

  • Engine Capacity: 313 cc
  • Weight: 175 kg
  • Transmission: 6 Speed Manual
  • Seat Height: 835 mm
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 11.5 litres
