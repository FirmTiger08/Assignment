The BMW G 310 R is a street bike available in only 1 variant and 4 colours. Powered by a 313cc BS6 engine, it develops a power of 33.52 bhp and a torque of 28 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, it comes equipped with an anti-lock braking system. Weighing 164 kg, it has a fuel tank capacity of 11 liters.

The 2023 model of the G 310 R features new styling updates, including new colours: Polar White with Racing Blue, Racing Red, and Cosmic Black 2. These updates are complemented by bold graphics while the design, features, and specifications remain unchanged.
